About Everproof

Everproof is a background screening and verification company, but unlike traditional screening offerings we turn the verified data into a continuous profile that can be shared with multiple third parties. Think of it like a verified LinkedIn profile which can be selectively shared by the owner.

Launched in February 2016, we are a growing, venture backed start-up that is striving to be one of the top tech companies in the world. We have ambitious plans and want to bring great people into the Everproof family.

The Role

We're looking for a UX Designer to be a core member of the Product Team. Your role will be to conduct user research, build highly functional and aesthetically pleasing interfaces, and test those interfaces with real users.

What We Need

  • Strong visual design skills - this is a must have
  • Demonstrated history of designing impactful user interfaces
  • Demonstrated ability to conduct and document user research
  • Experience building wireframes, mockups and prototypes
  • A broad understanding of modern web and mobile UI patterns
  • Experience with one of either Sketch or Photoshop
  • 3+ years of relevant experience, preferably in a start-up environment
  • Fast learner who can pick up unfamiliar concepts quickly
  • Enjoys, and has experience working in teams
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Goes above and beyond prescribed duties as and when required

What We Offer

  • Opportunity for rapid personal career growth as the company continues to grow
  • Fast-paced, exciting and fun startup environment
  • Working with a dedicated, passionate and mission driven team
  • High performance laptop and relevant software
  • Friday drinks, ice-cream trains, table tennis, and game nights at our Collingwood office
